About me

Things about me

Since my teenage days, I've always loved spending too much time on the computer. After getting my computer science degree, I realized writing compilers wasn't for me so decided to switch my path to writing/blogging. However, little did I know at that time, I'll be writing programs for web.

I've got chops in all phases of fullstack development process of an application.

Using technology, I enjoy helping clients and peers save time and money through online services and applications.

I love writing code, front and backend, both for work and fun. Interested in hybrid mobile applications, web-app security, and microservices.

Other pleasure I find is in reading books!
